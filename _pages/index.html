# Ogórek

## Etymology

From Proto-Slavic \*ogurъkъ

## Pronunciation

IPA(key): /ɔˈɡurɛk/

## Noun

ogórek m anim (diminutive ogóreczek, augmentative ogór)

1. cucumber
2. gherkin
